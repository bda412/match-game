A Match Made in Rescue Pup Heaven

Matching Game Rules

Click on the card to reveal an adorable dog on the other side. Click on a second card to try to find a match to the first. If you succeed, the pair witll be removed from play. If not, just try again!

How to Win

You win when all the matches have been found!
